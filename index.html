About Me

Hello! My name is Erik Hanson and I like to build all things software related and take on new and exciting challenges. I am a recent computer science graduate from Portland State University looking for software engineering positions! My love for programming came about when I started to solve logical problems in new ways that I never had using the power of programming.

My role there was fixing bugs and implementing features onto middletiers that the frontend would communicate with.
